Amplifying global musicians

Global musicians need work. Our mission is to give them work by encouraging organizations centered on Western classical music to incorporate global music into their concert programming.

See the Global Musician Advocate Awardees

What we do

  • {% include icons/site.html icon="population" %}

    Connect

    We cultivate relationships between global musicians and Western classical music presenters.

  • {% include icons/site.html icon="economy" %}

    Support

    By offering expertise and resources, we aid global musicians as they expand their professional careers.

  • {% include icons/site.html icon="conditions" %}

    Award

    Every season, organizations that hire global musicians receive the Global Musician Advocate Award.

As an Iranian-American composer working alongside Western and Iranian classical musicians of the highest caliber, I have seen firsthand the disparities in performance opportunities for global musicians. Through GMAP, I hope to create more opportunities for these musicians to share their first-rate artistry with audiences across the U.S.

Kian Ravaei, Composer & GMAP Founder

Global Musician Advocate Awardees

These organizations have demonstrated a commitment to supporting diverse musical traditions by hiring at least one global musician or ensemble during their concert season.
